SurfGI-GS: Deferred Global Illumination via 2D Gaussian Surfels In Progress

Victor Miene

Technical Report, 2026

We replace the volumetric 3D Gaussian primitives in GI-GS with 2D Gaussian surfels (Huang et al. 2024), resolving a fundamental architectural incompatibility between alpha-blended depth/normals and deferred shading's G-buffer contract. Surfels produce analytic per-pixel normals n = tu × tv and first-hit depth, fixing three cascading failures in the original pipeline: G-buffer normal corruption, depth-based position reconstruction error, and AO/indirect illumination degradation.
